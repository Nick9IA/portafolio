Biography

My journey as a web programmer began at the age of 32 when I was working for a logistics company as a manager, I had to develop strategies to optimize tasks. After a few years I decided to work in an ecommerce as an ATC supervisor and there I fell in love with how I could generate comprehensive solutions through programming. I am self-taught and have created solutions on my own, this has helped me to expand my range of knowledge and collect experiences through various disciplines, such as front-end development, print design, motion graphics, sculptures and 3D animations, photography, videography, psychology, and many more. Fast forward a few years, now I am focusing on product design, management, research, advice, leadership and entrepreneurship. My job is something I do with a lot of honesty, appetite and commitment. I am currently self-employed and I am working with a selected Independent customer base. I love new adventures, meeting new people and new challenges.
